Token Economics: Mo

Mo is the core token of MoVerse, used for governance, payments, and incentives.

  • Total Supply: 15 million Mo.
  • Allocation: 50% presale, 25% team, 25% ecosystem development.
  • Earning Methods: Trading rewards (40%), fiat exchange (40%), staking rewards (20%).
  • Fees: 3-6% transaction fees, with 40% burned to reduce supply.

Governance Structure

MoVerse is governed by the Mo Council with a dynamic structure:

  • Legislative Seats: Elected by top Mo holders and community votes.
  • Executive Seats: Composed of industry representatives, users, and AI.
  • Supervisory Seats: Audit nodes ensure transparency.
  • AI optimizes proposals, and NFT-based voting guarantees fairness.

Our Goals

MoVerse aims to lead the Web3 trading ecosystem:

  • Short-term (2025-2027): Achieve $5M in transactions.
  • Mid-term (2027-2030): Reach $50M in transactions.
  • Long-term (2030-2035): Surpass $500M in transactions.
