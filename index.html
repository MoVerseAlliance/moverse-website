Our Vision

MoVerse aims to redefine global trading through a decentralized ecosystem built on blockchain, AI, and NFTs. Starting with creative services in March 2025, we target a $2.5T market with 5% low fees, scaling to all legal trading scenarios by 2035.

Short-term (2025–2027)

Become the leading DAO in creative service trading with ¥100M annual transactions. Leverage AI-driven matching and low 5% fees to attract 10M designers and 5M employers, disrupting platforms like Fiverr (20% fees).

Mid-term (2027–2030)

Expand into mobility and delivery services, targeting ¥1B annual transactions. Integrate cross-scenario synergies (e.g., mobility + delivery) using AI optimization, capturing 5% of the $150B delivery market.

Long-term (2030–2035)

Achieve a "boundaryless trading" ecosystem covering all legal scenarios (e.g., logistics, leasing), with ¥10B annual transactions. Position MoVerse as a Web3 cornerstone, rivaling centralized giants with NFT-backed trust.

Governance

The Mo Council governs MoVerse with a dynamic, transparent structure: Legislative Seats for rule-making, Executive Seats for operations, and Supervisory Seats for audits. AI enhances decision-making, and NFT voting ensures fairness.

Legislative Seats

Top 5 Mo holders + Community votes

Functions: Set rules (e.g., 5% fees), amend charters, approve new scenarios (e.g., mobility). Elections quarterly via quadratic voting (e.g., 100 Mo = 10 votes). AI optimizes proposals, reducing invalid submissions by 50%.

Executive Seats

3 Industry reps + 2 Users + 1 AI

Functions: Manage daily operations, handle emergencies (e.g., pause trading if hacked), integrate scenarios. Elected semi-annually, impeached with 50% trust vote. AI drives cross-scenario efficiency (e.g., mobility + delivery).

Supervisory Seats

10-30 Audit nodes

Functions: Audit finances (e.g., ¥500M transactions), supervise rules, initiate impeachments. Elected annually, 51% to remove. Real-time dashboards track activity, with templates for new scenarios.

Token: Mo

Mo powers governance, payments, and incentives. Initial issuance: 15M Mo (50% presale, 25% team, 25% ecosystem). Earn via trading (40%), fiat exchange (40%), staking (20%). Fees 3-6%, 40% burned to control inflation.
