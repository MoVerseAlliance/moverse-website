Our Vision

MoVerse is a decentralized platform redefining global trading with blockchain, AI, and NFTs. Launching with creative services in March 2025, we aim to scale to all legal trading scenarios, offering 5% low fees and cutting-edge tech rooted in Eastern "Mo" culture.

Market Prospects

Creative Services Market

Expected global market size of $2.5T in 2025, with an 8% annual growth rate. Targeting 10M designers and 5M employers, capturing 1/3 of the market, with a transaction goal of ¥100M.

Mobility Services Market

Global mobility market exceeds $100B, targeting ¥50B in annual transactions, capturing 5%. Low-cost entry at 3% commission, competing with platforms like Uber.

Delivery Services Market

Food delivery market surpasses $150B, targeting ¥80B, capturing 5%. AI optimization improves delivery efficiency by 15%.

Governance

Mo Council governs MoVerse with a dynamic structure: Legislative Seats (top Mo holders + community votes), Executive Seats (industry reps + users + AI), and Supervisory Seats (audit nodes). AI optimizes proposals, and NFT-based voting ensures transparency.

Legislative Seats

  • Composition: Top 5 Mo holders (holding ≥ 1% of total circulation) and community votes, using Quadratic Voting (e.g., 100 Mo = 10 vote weight).
  • Dynamic Adjustment: For every ¥50M increase in transaction volume, add 1 seat, up to 15. New scenario launches nominate 1 industry representative (holding ≥ 5000 Mo).
  • Core Functions: Rule-making (e.g., 5% commission for creative services), charter amendments (e.g., adding seats), new scenario policies (e.g., approving "human resources" scenario).
  • Election: Quarterly, 3-month term, candidates stake 700 Mo, community votes decide.
  • Exit Conditions: Drop out of top 5 holders or miss 3 consecutive votes, automatic exit with 10% stake deduction (70 Mo).
  • Innovation: AI "MoCe" optimizes proposals, scenario incubator supports new scenarios (e.g., "education services" pilot).

Executive Seats

  • Composition: Initially 3 top designers (holding ≥ 2000 Mo), 2 top employers (holding ≥ 1500 Mo), 1 AI seat ("MoZhi"). Expands by 1 industry rep per 2 new scenarios, up to 12 seats.
  • Core Functions: Daily operations (e.g., 1500 daily creative tasks), emergency decisions (e.g., pause trading), scenario integration (e.g., mobility + delivery synergy).
  • Election: Semi-annually, 6-month term, candidates stake 1200 Mo, community votes, winners train for 5 days.
  • Exit Conditions: Inactive for 3 months or rule violation (e.g., misappropriation), 15% stake deduction (180 Mo).
  • Innovation: Cross-scenario synergy engine (e.g., 18% efficiency increase in mobility + transport), dynamic seat allocation (based on transaction volume).

Supervisory Seats

  • Composition: Initially 10 audit nodes (holding ≥ 800 Mo), add 5 nodes per ¥50M transaction volume increase, up to 30, randomly selected.
  • Core Functions: Financial audits (e.g., liquidity pool), rule supervision (e.g., compliance), impeachment (8 nodes initiate, 51% vote to pass).
  • Election: Annually in December, 12-month term, >25% veto rate triggers reselection.
  • Exit Conditions: Error rate >10% or fraud, full stake deduction, 30-day freeze.
  • Innovation: Real-time audit dashboard (15-minute updates), scenario templates (e.g., transport: mileage + fee).

Token: Mo

Mo is the ecosystem's token for governance, payments, and incentives. Initial issuance: 15M Mo (50% presale, 25% team, 25% ecosystem). Earn via trading rewards (40%), fiat exchange (40%), and staking (20%). Fees range 3-6%, with 40% burned.

Token Acquisition

  • Trading Rewards (40%): Creative services: 6% commission + 3% reward, extended scenarios: 3-6% commission + 1-2% reward, daily distribution on Polygon, reward cap at 150K Mo, 10% bonus for cross-scenario trades.
  • Fiat Exchange (40%): Initially 1 Mo = 1 CNY, 0.7% fee, daily cap at 3M Mo, supports multi-currency exchange (USDT, ETH).
  • Staking Rewards (20%): 7% annualized return, staking pool of 600K Mo, scenario bonus +2% (e.g., 9% for creative services staking).

Token Consumption

  • Proposal: Regular proposal: 50 Mo, charter/scenario expansion: 500 Mo.
  • Upgrade: System upgrades: 250-900 Mo (e.g., DApp upgrade: 500 Mo).
  • Commission: Transaction fees: 3-6% (e.g., ¥1000 task, 50 Mo commission).
  • Traffic Fee: Task prioritization: 60-200 Mo (e.g., 18-hour top listing: 60 Mo).
  • Arbitration Fee: Initiate arbitration: 5% Mo, first appeal doubles (10% Mo), second appeal triples (15% Mo).
  • Cross-Scenario Fee: Cross-scenario trades: +0.3% Mo (e.g., ¥1000 order, extra 3 Mo).

Inflation Control

  • Issuance: Base issuance at 1.5%, AI-adjusted up to 4%.
  • Burn: 40% of transaction fees burned, daily cap at 100K Mo, excess accumulates.
  • Initial Issuance: 15M Mo: 50% presale (7.5M Mo, ¥5.25M), 25% team (5-year unlock, 3.75M Mo), 25% ecosystem (3.75M Mo).
