Transaction Flow

1. Post Demand

Employer A posts a ¥2000 UI design task with a 7-day deadline, paying 10 Mo fee, stored on IPFS and Polygon.

2. AI Matching

AI matches 6 designers in 8 minutes based on ratings (40%), skills (40%), and domain (20%), scoring 75+.

3. Bidding (Dark + Open)

Designers bid in a 6-minute dark pool (e.g., ¥1800, 6 days), followed by open confirmation. Lowest bid wins (AI mean: ¥130/hour).

4. Delivery & Acceptance

Designer B delivers via IPFS, Employer A accepts within 30 hours, rating 5 stars. Funds (1710 Mo) released.

5. NFT Credential

NFT minted (#001, ¥1800, 2025-03-01) as proof, tradable at 40 Mo or used as credit.
